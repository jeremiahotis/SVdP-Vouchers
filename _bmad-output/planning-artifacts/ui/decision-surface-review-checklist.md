# Decision Surface Review Checklist (v1)

**Used in:** Shyft Review Phase  
**Purpose:** Validate Decision Surface compliance

---

## Structural Order

- [ ] Context (read-only) appears before inputs
- [ ] Identity is established before situation
- [ ] Situation details are progressively revealed
- [ ] Decision is presented as a choice (Decision Cards)
- [ ] Steward confirmation appears last

---

## Decision Clarity

- [ ] Exactly one primary decision exists
- [ ] Primary CTA is visually dominant
- [ ] No competing CTAs or parallel actions

---

## Visual Discipline

- [ ] Only approved design tokens are used
- [ ] Accent color used only for decision states
- [ ] Spacing follows 4/8 rhythm
- [ ] Elevation does not exceed two levels

---

## Copy & Tone

- [ ] No emotional framing
- [ ] No preemptive policy explanation
- [ ] Language meets Grade 8 readability

---

## Accessibility

- [ ] Keyboard navigable
- [ ] Focus visible at all times
- [ ] Tap targets meet minimum size for PWA

---

## Outcome

If any item fails:
- Surface is **not approved**
- Changes must be made or exception documented

This checklist is enforceable, not advisory.
